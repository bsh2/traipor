Please note:
The plans generated by this service aren't training plans meant to be followed
by you, the user.
They are just simulations predicting the future based on mathematical models.
These models don't take your state of health into account in any way. Decide
for yourself or ask your trainer how to incorporate the generated
simulation results into your personal training schedule. By all means, don't
overstrain your body just because some numbers in a simulation tell you so.
Consult a medical doctor to ensure your healthiness and ability to train.

The provider will only collect, use and pass on personal data if permitted by law or the user complies with the collection.

Cookies

Cookies are small files that make it possible to store device specific information on the device of the user (PC, phone, etc.). They are used for the user-friendliness of websites (e.g. storing of login data). They also serve to collect statistical data about the useage of a website, to analyze and improve the service. Users can influence the use of cookies. Most browsers offer an option to restrict or disable the storage of cookies. However, it is pointed out that the use and more particularly the comfort without cookies are limited.
